Question: I need to set up my new delivery address

Answer: Assuredly! I'm here to guide you through setting up your new delivery address. Here's a step-by-step process:

1. Log in to your account on our website or app.
2. Navigate to the "My Account" or "Profile" section.
3. Look for the "Shipping Addresses" or similar tab.
4. There should be an option to "Add a New Address" or "Edit Shipping Addresses". Click on it.
5. Fill in the required fields with the details of your new delivery address, such as street name, city, state, and zip code.
6. Make sure to double-check the information for accuracy.
7. Click on the "Save" or "Submit" button to save your new delivery address.

If you encounter any issues or have any questions during the process, don't hesitate to reach out. We're more than happy to assist you further.
