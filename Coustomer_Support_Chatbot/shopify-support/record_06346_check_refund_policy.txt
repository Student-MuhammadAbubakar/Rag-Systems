Question: I want to see in which cases can I request my money back

Answer: Definitely! Understanding the scenarios in which you can request a refund is crucial. Here's a breakdown of the situations that generally warrant a refund:

1. **Product/Service Defect:** If the product or service you purchased is defective or doesn't meet your expectations, you usually have the right to request a refund.
2. **Cancellation within Grace Period:** If you change your mind and decide to cancel your purchase within the specified grace period, a refund is often available.
3. **Unauthorized Charges:** If you notice charges on your account that you didn't authorize or recognize, our team will investigate and assist you in resolving the issue.

5. **Duplicate Charges:** If you find that you have been charged multiple times for the same item, please let us know so that we can rectify the situation and provide a refund.
6. **Non-receipt of Goods:** If you haven't received the product within the expected timeframe, you may be entitled to a refund.
