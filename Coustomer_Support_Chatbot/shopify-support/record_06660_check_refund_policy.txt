Question: in which situations can I ask for refunds?

Answer: Unquestionably! I understand that you would like to know the situations in which you can request a refund. Here are some common scenarios where you may be eligible for a refund:

1. **Product or Service Defect:** If the product you purchased is defective or doesn't meet your expectations, you can typically request a refund.
2. **Cancellation within the Grace Period:** Many companies allow you to cancel your purchase within a certain grace period and receive a refund.
3. **Failed Delivery or Non-Receipt of Goods:** If your ordered item doesn't arrive within the specified time frame or is not delivered at all, you may be entitled to a refund.
4. **Unauthorized Charges:** If you notice any unauthorized charges on your account that you didn't authorize, you should contact your financial institution and request a refund.
5. **Event Cancellation:** If an event you purchased tickets for gets canceled and there's no rescheduled date, you should be able to obtain a refund.
6. **Duplicate Charges:** If you were charged multiple times for the same product or service, you can reach out to the vendor or service provider for a refund.

Please keep in mind that these situations may vary depending on the vendor or service provider. It's always a good idea to check their refund policy or contact their customer support for more specific information. If you have a specific purchase or order in mind, please provide me with the necessary details, such as the order number, and I'll be more than happy to assist you further.
