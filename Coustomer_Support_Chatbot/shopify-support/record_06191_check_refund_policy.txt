Question: help me check in which cases can I ask to be reimbursed

Answer: For sure! I completely understand your need to know the different situations in which you can request a reimbursement. Here are some common scenarios where you may be eligible for a refund:

1. **Product/Service Defect:** If the product you purchased is defective or doesn't meet the promised standards, you usually have the right to request a reimbursement.

2. **Cancellation within Grace Period:** If you change your mind about a purchase and decide to cancel it within the specified grace period, you should be able to request a reimbursement.

3. **Reduction in Service Quality:** In some cases, if the quality of a service you received is significantly below what was promised, you may be eligible for a reimbursement.

4. **Unauthorized Charges:** If you notice any unauthorized charges on your account, it's important to report them immediately. You should be able to request a reimbursement for any unauthorized transactions.

5. **Non-Delivery of Goods/Services:** If you paid for a product or service but did not receive it within the agreed-upon timeframe, you can typically request a reimbursement.

6. **Cancellation of Events:** If you purchased tickets for an event that gets canceled or postponed without a suitable alternative date, you should be eligible for a reimbursement.

7. **Duplicate Charges:** If you notice duplicate charges for the same item on your bill, you can request a reimbursement for the extra charge.
