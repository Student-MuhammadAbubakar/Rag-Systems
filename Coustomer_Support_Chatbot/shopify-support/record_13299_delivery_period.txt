Question: how do I check how long it takes for my package to arrive?

Answer: We understand your desire to track the progress and estimated arrival time of your package. To provide you with the most accurate information, we recommend checking the tracking status of your package. You can usually find the tracking number in the confirmation email or on the order details page. Once you have the tracking number, you can visit our website or use our mobile app to enter the tracking number and see the real-time updates on your package's whereabouts. If you have any difficulty finding the tracking information or need further assistance, our customer support team is available to help you. Feel free to reach out to them through the live chat on our website or by calling our dedicated support hotline. We hope this helps you stay informed about your package's delivery progress.
