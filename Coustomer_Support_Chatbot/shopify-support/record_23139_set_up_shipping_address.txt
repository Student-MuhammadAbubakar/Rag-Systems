Question: can i enter a secondary shipping address

Answer: Certainly! You can definitely enter a secondary shipping address. This will allow you to have multiple options for delivery locations. To add a secondary shipping address, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your account.
2. Navigate to the 'My Account' or 'Profile' section.
3. Look for the 'Shipping Addresses' or similar section.
4. There should be an option to 'Add a New Address' or 'Edit Shipping Addresses'. Select it.
5. Fill in the details of your secondary shipping address in the provided fields.
6. Don't forget to save the changes.
