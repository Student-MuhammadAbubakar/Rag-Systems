Question: I want to check in what situations can I request a refund

2. **Cancellation within Grace Period:** Should you have a change of heart or need to cancel your order within the specified grace period, you would typically be eligible for a refund.

4. **Event Cancellation:** If you purchased tickets for an event and it gets canceled without a rescheduled date, you would generally be entitled to a refund.

5. **Duplicate Charges:** If you have been charged more than once for the same item or service, please inform us promptly. We will rectify the error and process a refund for the extra charges.

6. **Non-receipt of Goods:** If you haven't received your ordered product within the expected delivery timeframe, kindly reach out to us. We will investigate the delay and, if the situation warrants, provide a refund.
