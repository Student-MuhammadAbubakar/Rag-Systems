Question: my adddress changed how can i update it

Answer: Sure! If your address has changed, you can easily update it by following these steps:

1. Log in to your account on our website.
2. Go to the "Account Settings" or "Profile" section.
3. Look for the option to change your shipping address.
4. Click on it and enter your new address details, including the street, city, state, and zip code.
5. Save the changes and make sure to double-check all the information for accuracy.

Once you update your address, our system will automatically use the new address for future deliveries. If you have any further questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team.
