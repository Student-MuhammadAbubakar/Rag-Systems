Question: under which circumstances can I ask for my money back?

Answer: I'll take care of it! I completely understand your need to know the circumstances under which you can request a refund and get your money back. Here are some common situations where you might be eligible for a refund:

1. **Product/Service Defect:** If the product or service you purchased is defective, doesn't match the description, or doesn't meet your expectations, you can typically request a refund.

2. **Cancellation within Grace Period:** If you change your mind about a purchase and cancel it within the specified grace period, you should be eligible for a refund.

3. **Delivery Issues:** If your order doesn't arrive within the expected time frame or gets lost in transit, you have a valid reason to request a refund.

4. **Unauthorized Charges:** If you notice any charges on your account that you didn't authorize or if you detect fraudulent activity, you should contact us immediately to investigate the situation and request a refund if necessary.

5. **Event Cancellation:** If you purchase tickets for an event that gets canceled, postponed indefinitely, or rescheduled, you may be entitled to a refund.

6. **Duplicate Charges:** If you notice duplicate charges for the same item on your billing statement, please let us know, and we will work on resolving the issue and refund any excess charges.

7. **Change in Circumstances:** If there's a significant change in circumstances that prevents you from using the product or service as intended, such as a medical condition or relocation, you may be eligible for a refund.
