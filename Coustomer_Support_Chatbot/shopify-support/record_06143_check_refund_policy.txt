5. **Duplicate Charges:** If you notice duplicate charges for the same transaction on your account, please let us know. We'll investigate and resolve the issue.
6. **Non-delivery of Goods:** If you haven't received the product you ordered within the expected delivery timeframe, you may be eligible for a refund.
