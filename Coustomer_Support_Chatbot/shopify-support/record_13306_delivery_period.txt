Question: where can I check how soon can I expect the item?

Answer: We understand your desire to track the progress of your item and have visibility on its estimated arrival time. To check the estimated delivery date, you can refer to the confirmation email or SMS that was sent to you when the item was dispatched. Alternatively, you can log in to your account on our website and navigate to the "Order History" or "Track My Order" section. There, you will find all the relevant information regarding your order, including the estimated delivery date. If you have any difficulties locating this information, our customer support team is available to assist you.
