Question: what do i need to do to submit the new shipping address

Answer: To submit the new shipping address, please follow these steps:

1. Log in to your account.
2. Navigate to the 'My Account' or 'Profile' section.
3. Look for the 'Shipping Addresses' or similar section.
4. Select the option to 'Add a New Address' or 'Edit Shipping Addresses'.
5. Fill in the details of the new shipping address in the provided fields.
6. Double-check the accuracy of the entered information.
7. Save the changes.

Once you have completed these steps, your new shipping address will be submitted and used for future deliveries. If you encounter any issues or have further questions, feel free to let me know, and I'll be glad to assist you further.
