Question: I do not know what to do to edit the shipping address

Answer: I apologize for any confusion. To update your shipping address, please follow these steps: 

1. Log in to your account on our website/app.
2. Go to the "My Account" or "Profile" section.
3. Look for the "Shipping Address" or "Delivery Information" tab.
4. Click on it to edit your current address.
5. Make the necessary changes and click on the "Save" or "Update" button.

If you encounter any difficulties or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support.
