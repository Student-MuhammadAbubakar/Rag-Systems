Question: I don't know how to check when my aprcel is going to arrive

Answer: We understand that tracking the delivery time of your parcel is important to you. Allow me to guide you through the process. To check the estimated arrival date of your parcel, you can visit our website and log in to your account. Once logged in, navigate to the "Order History" or "Track Order" section. There you will find detailed information about your order, including the expected delivery date. If you do not have an account, you can also track your parcel using the tracking number provided in the confirmation email. Simply enter the tracking number on our website's tracking page or through the courier's tracking system. Both methods will provide you with the most up-to-date information on the status and delivery date of your parcel. If you encounter any difficulties or have any further questions, please feel free to let us know. We are here to assist you at every step of the way.
