Question: where to check how long it takes for my delivery to arrive

Answer: To check the estimated delivery time for your package, you can refer to the tracking information provided to you. You should have received a tracking number or an order number when you placed your order. Using this number, you can visit our website or the courier's website and enter the tracking information in the designated tracking section. This will give you real-time updates on the location and the expected delivery date of your parcel. If you need any further assistance or have any other questions, please feel free to let us know.
