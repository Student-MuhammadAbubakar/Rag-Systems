Question: what do I have to do to see my compensation current status?

Answer: In order to check the current status of your compensation, you can follow these steps:

1. Log in to your account on our website/mobile app.
2. Navigate to the "My Account" or "My Orders" section.
3. Look for the specific order or transaction for which you are expecting compensation.
4. Click on the relevant order or transaction to view its details.
5. Within the order or transaction details, you should find information about the status of your compensation.

If you are unable to find the information or have any difficulties, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team. They will be more than happy to assist you further with checking the status of your compensation.
