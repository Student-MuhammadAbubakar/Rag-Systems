1. **Product/Service Defect:** If you receive a defective product or the service provided doesn't meet your expectations, you usually have the right to request a reimbursement.

2. **Late Delivery:** In the event that your order doesn't arrive within the expected timeframe, you may be eligible for a reimbursement.

3. **Cancellation within Grace Period:** Should you change your mind about a purchase within the specified grace period, you can typically ask for a reimbursement.

4. **Unauthorized Charges:** If you notice any unexpected charges on your account or credit card statement, reaching out to the appropriate parties can help resolve the issue and potentially lead to a reimbursement.

5. **Non-receipt of Goods/Services:** If you haven't received the goods or services you paid for, even after a reasonable amount of time has passed, you can usually request a reimbursement.

6. **Misrepresentation or False Advertising:** If a product or service was advertised falsely, and as a result, you made a purchase that didn't meet your expectations, you may be eligible for a reimbursement.
