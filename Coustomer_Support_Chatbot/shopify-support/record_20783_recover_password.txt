Question: I lost my user password, would it be possible to recover it?

Answer: Unquestionably! I'm here to guide you through the process of recovering your user password and helping you regain access to your account.

To get started, please follow these steps:

1. Visit the login page of our platform.
2. Look for the "Forgot Password" option and click on it.
3. You will be prompted to enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Once you've entered the email address, a password reset email will be sent to that address. Please check your inbox, and don't forget to look in your spam or junk folder as well, just in case it gets filtered there.
5. Open the password reset email and follow the instructions provided to set up a new password for your account.

If you encounter any issues during this process or have any further questions, please feel free to reach out to me. I'm here to assist you every step of the way and ensure that you regain access to your account successfully.
