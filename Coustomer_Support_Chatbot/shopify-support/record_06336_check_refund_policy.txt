2. **Cancellation within Grace Period:** Many companies offer a grace period during which you can cancel your purchase and receive a refund without any penalties.

4. **Unauthorized Charges:** If you notice any unauthorized charges on your account, you have the right to dispute them and request a refund.

5. **Failure to Deliver Goods or Services:** If the company fails to deliver the goods or services you paid for, you are entitled to a refund.

6. **Product Out of Stock:** If you ordered a product and it is out of stock or unavailable, the company should offer you a refund or an alternative solution.
