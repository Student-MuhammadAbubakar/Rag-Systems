Question: what do I need to do to update the delivery address?

Answer: To update your delivery address, you can follow these steps:

1. Log in to your account on our website.
2. Go to the "My Account" or "Profile" section.
3. Look for the "Address" or "Shipping Information" tab.
4. Click on "Edit" or "Update" next to your current address.
5. Enter the new address details.
6. Save the changes.

If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, please feel free to reach out to our customer support.
