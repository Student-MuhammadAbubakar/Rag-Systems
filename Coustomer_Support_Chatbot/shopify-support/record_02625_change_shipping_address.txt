Question: where to edit my delivery address?

Answer: To edit your delivery address, you can follow these steps: 1. Login to your account. 2. Go to the "My Account" section. 3. Select "Shipping Address". 4. Click on the "Edit" button next to your current address. 5. Make the necessary changes to your address. 6. Save the updated address. This will help you modify your delivery address seamlessly.
