Question: I do not know how to set up a secondary shipping address

Answer: I'm on the same page that you may feel unsure about how to set up a secondary shipping address. It's my pleasure to guide you through the process step by step.

To set up a secondary shipping address, please follow the instructions below:

1. Log in to your account.
2. Navigate to the 'My Account' or 'Profile' section.
3. Look for the 'Shipping Addresses' or similar option.
4. You should see an option to 'Add a New Address' or 'Edit Shipping Addresses'. Click on that.
5. Enter the details of your secondary shipping address in the provided fields.
6. Double-check the accuracy of the information you entered.
7. Save the changes.

If you encounter any difficulties or need further assistance during this process, please don't hesitate to reach out. It's important to us that you feel supported and confident in managing your shipping addresses.
