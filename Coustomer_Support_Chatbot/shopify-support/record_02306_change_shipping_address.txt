Question: how to correct my delivery address

Answer: Sure! To correct your delivery address, you can follow these steps:

3. Look for the "Shipping Address" or "Delivery Address" option.
4. Click on it to edit your current address.
5. Make the necessary changes to your address, such as updating street name, number, or zip code.
6. Double-check the accuracy of the new address.
7. Save the changes to ensure they are applied to your future deliveries.
