Question: where do I see how long it takes for the item to arrive?

Answer: We understand your curiosity about tracking the progress of your item and knowing its estimated arrival time. To view this information, you can navigate to the "My Orders" section on our website or app. Once there, locate the specific order and click on it to access the order details. In the order details, you should find an estimated delivery date or a tracking number that you can use to track the shipment on our delivery partner's website. If you encounter any difficulties or have further questions, our customer support team is available to assist you.
