Question: what do I have to do to set up another delivery address?

Answer: To set up another delivery address, you can follow these steps:

1. Log in to your account.
2. Navigate to the 'My Account' or 'Profile' section.
3. Look for the 'Shipping Addresses' or similar section.
4. Select the option to 'Add a New Address' or 'Edit Shipping Addresses'.
5. Enter the details of the new delivery address in the provided fields.
6. Save the changes.
