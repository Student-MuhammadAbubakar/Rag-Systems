Question: how do I correct my delivery address?

Answer: We understand the importance of keeping your delivery address up to date. To update your delivery address, please follow these simple steps:

1. Log in to your account on our website.
2. Go to the "My Account" section.
3. Find the "Delivery Address" tab and click on it.
4. Edit the existing address or enter a new one.
5. Save your changes.

If you encounter any difficulties or have any further questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team. We are here to assist you.
