Question: I want to see in which cases can I ask for reimbursements

Answer: Thank you for reaching out. I completely understand your desire to have clarity on the cases where you can request reimbursements. Let me provide you with a comprehensive breakdown:

2. **Cancellation within Grace Period:** If you change your mind and decide to cancel your purchase within the specified grace period, you should be entitled to a refund.
3. **Unauthorized Charges:** If you notice any charges on your account that you did not authorize or recognize, we will certainly investigate and assist you in resolving the issue.
4. **Event Cancellation:** If you have purchased tickets for an event that gets canceled with no rescheduled date, you would normally qualify for a refund.
5. **Duplicate Charges:** If you are charged multiple times for the same item or service, please let us know so that we can promptly correct the error and issue a refund if needed.
6. **Non-receipt of Goods:** If you have not received a product within the expected timeframe, you may be eligible for a refund.

To have a comprehensive understanding, it is advisable to consult directly with them or review their terms and conditions.
