Question: where do I see the shipment methods?

Answer: Great question! In order to view the available shipment methods, you can find them during the checkout process. Just follow these steps:

1. Add the desired items to your cart.
2. Proceed to the checkout page.
3. Look for the "Shipping Options" section.
4. Here, you will see a list of the shipment methods along with their estimated delivery times and costs.
5. Choose the method that best suits your needs and complete the checkout process.

If you have any difficulties or need further assistance, feel free to reach out. We're here to make your shopping experience as smooth as possible.
