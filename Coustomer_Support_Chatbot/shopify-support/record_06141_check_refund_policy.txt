2. **Cancellation within Refund Window:** If you change your mind and decide to cancel your purchase within the specified refund window, you should be able to request a refund.

5. **Event Cancellation:** If you purchased tickets for an event that was canceled and not rescheduled, you are typically eligible for a refund.

6. **Duplicate Charges:** If you were charged multiple times for the same product or service, you can request a refund for the duplicate charges.
