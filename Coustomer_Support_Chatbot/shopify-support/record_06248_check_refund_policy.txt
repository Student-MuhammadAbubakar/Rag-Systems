Answer: I understand your need to know the specific cases in which you can request a refund. Here are some common scenarios where you may be eligible:

2. **Cancellation within Grace Period:** Many companies offer a grace period during which you can cancel your purchase and receive a refund.

6. **Non-receipt of Goods:** If you haven't received the product within the expected delivery timeframe, you may be eligible for a refund.
