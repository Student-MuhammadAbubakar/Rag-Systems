Question: my delivery address has changed how can i modify it

Answer: We apologize for the inconvenience caused. To modify your delivery address, please follow these steps:

1. Log in to your account on our website.
2. Navigate to the "My Account" section.
3. Look for the "Address Book" or "Shipping Address" option.
4. Click on it and select the address you want to modify.
5. Make the necessary changes to the address fields.
6. Save the updated address.

If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team. We are here to help!
